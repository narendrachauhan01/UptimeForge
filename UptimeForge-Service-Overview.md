## What We Monitor

### Website Uptime Monitoring
Checks your website every 30 seconds to 5 minutes (depending on plan). If it goes down or returns an error, you get an instant alert. When it recovers, you get a recovery alert too.

### Ping / Host Monitoring
Monitors raw servers, IP addresses, and network ports directly. Ideal for monitoring database servers, game servers, mail servers, or any TCP-based service — not just websites.

### SSL Certificate Alerts
Automatically checks your HTTPS certificate expiry date. Sends you reminders **30 days, 14 days, and 7 days** before it expires. An expired SSL certificate shows a scary "Not Secure" warning to your visitors and kills trust instantly.

### Domain Expiry Alerts
Reminds you before your domain name expires. Forgetting to renew your domain means your entire website goes offline — UptimeForge makes sure that never happens.

### Response Time Tracking
Tracks how fast your website loads over time. Slow websites lose customers — Google reports that 53% of mobile users abandon a page that takes longer than 3 seconds to load. Our charts help you spot slowdowns before they become a problem.
